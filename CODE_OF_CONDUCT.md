# Contributor Code of Conduct

PhyloAnnotator is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free
community. Contributors are expected to communicate with empathy, assume good
faith, and focus critique on ideas and implementation details.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal
attacks, publishing private information, and sustained disruption.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when
behavior undermines the community.
